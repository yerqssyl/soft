Rehabilitation of motor functions

Rehabilitation of motor functions

The online exercises and tasks that we offer for motor recovery after stroke allow you to stimulate and rehabilitate some of the main functions affected by apoplexy, such as motor, vestibular functions, as well as muscle strengthening and hand motility.

We offer a 3-step treatment approach. All stages are three months old. The first stage is the stage when the patient is helped by a caregiver or a close person in the implementation of the exercises. The second stage is the stage when the patient performs independent exercises, but does not forget about the exercises with the help of loved ones. The third stage, the patient performs the exercises completely himself.

I.U.H will provide individual exercises tailored to your body. And an individual schedule / calendar in which you will see your results. And remember your main motto - "I'm up the hill"!

Rehabilitation of cognitive functions

The online exercises and assignments offered by I.U.H for cognitive recovery from stroke allow to stimulate and rehabilitate some of the main functions affected by apoplexy, such as hand-eye coordination, memory for names, perception, memory, attention or planning ability.

Rehabilitation of cognitive functions

Patients who receive early cognitive rehabilitation after a stroke or apoplexy are more responsive to treatment, which can reduce and restore functions affected by brain damage.

For optimal results, it is recommended that this therapeutic protocol be followed 3 times a week for at least 6 months. Each cognitive rehabilitation session lasts approximately 20 minutes. During this time, I.U.H will automatically suggest a series of specific, personalized tasks, adapted to the cognitive needs of each patient. The level of difficulty of the exercises will adapt to what is needed as the person trains.

Nutrition

What you eat has an effect on how your body functions, including your heart.

Nutritional therapy is one of the main directions in recovery after myocardial infarction. The diet, the range of dishes and the technology of their preparation depend on the patient's condition, the duration of his stay in bed and the presence of concomitant diseases. You should consult your doctor about the features of the post-infarction diet.

All bad habits should be excluded, in particular alcohol, tobacco products, etc. It will also be necessary to replace saturated fats in the diet with polyunsaturated fatty acids, which are found in vegetable oils.

also another enemy of the heart is salt. It is known that excessive consumption of salty foods leads to the development of arterial hypertension and other cardiovascular diseases. If you consume more than 5 g of salt per day, the risk of heart and vascular disease increases by 17%, and the risk of stroke - by 23% 7.

Боковое движение запястьем Это последнее упражнение для рук уровня 1. Оно обеспечивает восстановление движения в запястье в направлении из стороны в сторону. Положите пораженную руку на стол ладонью вниз. Затем используйте свою здоровую руку, чтобы двигать парализованной рукой влево, затем вправо. Повторяйте медленно из стороны в сторону.

9 из 10

Скользящие движения. Для этого более сложного упражнения для рук поместите бутылку с водой в пораженную руку. Держите руку и пальцы расслабленными. Согните пальцы и возьмите бутылку с водой в руку. Затем отпустите обратно вниз. Повторите движение, продолжая медленно открывать и закрывать пальцы, чтобы мозг успел распознать и переучить это движение. Это упражнение позволит укрепить мышцы, которые позволяют удерживать запястье.

10 из 10

Сгибание пальцев в «колечки». В последнем упражнении для руки попробуйте коснуться кончиком каждого пальца большого пальца, делая из двух пальцев «колечко». Начиная с указательного пальца, переходите к среднему пальцу, затем к безымянному пальцу и, наконец, к мизинцу. Клинически доказано, что это упражнение помогает улучшить функцию руки у пациентов с инсультом всего за 2 недели.
